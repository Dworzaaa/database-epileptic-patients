Database of patients in epilepsy research

This project is being created by students of bachelor study programme at Czech Technical University in Prague, Faculty of Information Technology who are cooperating with Faculty of Electrical Engineering. The client is the University hospital Motol.

Welcome to Database of patients in epilepsy research page.

Anotation, as well as this whole document, will be continuously updated, depending upon the details that team will get from the client and the supervisor. The expected release date of the first version is the end of the year 2013. For more details about this project, please check the repo and stay tuned. Pages are under construction in this moment.

Used technologies and architecture

Backend will be running on Java EE + Spring MVC framework. As an application server will be used Apache Tomcat 7 or JBoss 7. Frontend will be realized via JSP+CSS+js. As OS will be used some linux distribution - hot candidates are Debian, CentOS and Fedora. We'll use MySQL database.
